# Adidas Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Adidas sales data to uncover key business insights, track performance trends, and evaluate profitability across products, regions, and sales channels.

The objective was to transform raw sales data into an interactive dashboard using Power BI to support strategic decision-making and performance monitoring.

---

## Business Problem
Adidas management needed a clear view of sales performance across different dimensions, including:

- Product categories  
- Geographic regions  
- Sales channels (In-store, Outlet, Online)  
- Time (monthly & quarterly trends)  

The goal was to identify top-performing areas, detect growth opportunities, and improve profitability.

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains sales data for 2020–2021, including:

- Product Category  
- Region  
- Sales Method  
- Units Sold  
- Price per Unit  
- Total Sales  
- Operating Profit  
- Date (Year & Month)  

The dataset was structured and required transformation and modeling for effective analysis.

---

## Key Findings

### Top Performing Product
Men’s Street Footwear was identified as the best-selling product category, generating the highest revenue among all segments.

### Regional Performance
The West region recorded the highest sales (~$270M), making it the strongest geographic contributor.

### Sales Channel Analysis
- In-store sales contributed the largest share (~40%)  
- Outlet and Online channels followed  
- Each channel showed different profitability patterns  

### Sales Growth & Trends
- Achieved **394% Year-over-Year growth**  
- Peak sales recorded in **July 2021**  
- Strong upward trend observed in 2021 compared to 2020  

### Profitability Insights
- Overall profit margin reached **36.9%**  
- Variation in margins across product categories highlighted optimization opportunities  

---

## Dashboard Features
The interactive dashboard provides:

- Total Sales, Total Units Sold, and Operating Profit KPIs  
- Sales breakdown by Product and Region  
- Sales distribution by Channel  
- Monthly & Quarterly trend analysis  
- Profit margin and performance indicators  
- Dynamic filters (State, Region, Product)  
- Drill-down capabilities for deeper insights
